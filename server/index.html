What is Chatdata.nl?

Chatdata.nl is a ChatGPT plugin that enables access to CBS (Centraal Bureau voor de Statistiek) Open Data. By integrating this plugin, journalists, researchers, and other interested parties can effortlessly explore the vast amount of data provided by CBS and generate valuable insights.

Key Features:

  • Seamless integration with ChatGPT
  • Access to a wide range of CBS Open Data
  • Easy-to-use interface for data exploration

Possible Use Cases:

  1. Trend Analysis: Journalists can use Chatdata.nl to identify and analyze trends in various social, economic, and environmental indicators, such as unemployment rates, housing prices, and pollution levels.
  2. Data-driven Storytelling: By utilizing the ChatGPT plugin, journalists and researchers can create data-driven stories and articles that offer valuable insights and engage readers.
  3. Policy Analysis: Chatdata.nl allows policy analysts and other interested researchers to access and evaluate the impact of different policies on various aspects of society and the economy.
